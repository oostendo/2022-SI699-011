Modeling Principal Building Activity

We hypothesize there is a significant difference in the energy use intensity and presence of a water heater booster. Results show that the differences are statistically significant (p = 0.0099). Thus, we modeled this building characteristic with energy usage intensity using OLS Linear Regression. Unfortunately, this extremely simplified linear regression model does not explain 3% of the variance in the data. In the future, performing feature selection of the original variables may help improve model performance and make output and predictions more easily interpretable.
